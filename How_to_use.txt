Here is how to use the application.

Prerequisites:
- A web-cam must be plugged in to your computer

Video capture:
- To start the video-stream, press the button "Start" located at the bottom of the window.
- To stop the video-stream, press the button "Stop" located at the bottom of the window.

Handling views:
- One can simply toggle views by clicking the "Views" tab in the top-menubar.
- Each view can be dragged to suit the users needs by hovering the mouse between the views.
- It is also possible to close views by dragging them.
